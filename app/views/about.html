ABOUT

My name is Allon Jacobs and I’m an instrument rated private pilot in California. I attribute my flying addiction to my dad who let me ride along as he was doing unusual attitudes in a Cessna 210 over Los Angeles when I was just a few years old. A few decades later, I found an opportunity to pour every last cent from my summer construction job into flight lessons at the University of California, Davis. Sound familiar?


More recently, I realized when traveling domestically and internationally that I was having difficulty finding other pilots to speak with and challenged with discovering new flying experiences. On one occasion, it literally took a referral from a surf shop owner, a broken conversation in Spanish with a bartender, a wild goose chase to unmarked beaches, and all to find some flying ultralight pilot that was rumored to give flights over Costa Rica. That's what sparked the idea to create a community where people could find one another and connect.


Pilot Connect is a site for pilots to find the hidden gems of flight experiences waiting to be uncovered, to expand connections with one another, as a forum to ask questions, and get inspired with great stories. Whether you’re going to Montana and want to take a shot at flying float planes, offering advice on how to clear the challenging airspace in your area, giving a local flight tour of your city, or even finding a Safety Pilot, this is your one stop shop.


Any and all feedback is welcome on our Contact Us page. Seriously, while it can sometimes take some time, I read every single submission!
